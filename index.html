SOUND

Water (2025)

Water explores the relationship between natural and human experiences of water. The work captures the subtle sound of rivers and waterfalls, reflecting the quiet presence of nature.

In contrast, it introduces the sounds of water shaped by everyday human activity, separated into the left channel (natural water) and the right channel (human use), creating a dialogue between the organic and the constructed.

Through this contrast, Hayley Zhang presents an exploration of how water exists both as a vital resource and as a source of calm and reflection.

Listen and Breath (2026)

Listen and Breath is a meditative sound composition by Hayley Zhang, developed as part of her meditation workshop and collective sessions presented in her Fine Art BA First Year Show.

As a continuation of Water, the work extends her exploration of sound, awareness, and inner experience.

Through gentle, sustained tones, it invites audiences into a shared moment of stillness and reflection.

ABOUT

Hayley Zhang is a meditation music creator and artist focused on sound-based relaxation and inner awareness. Her work combines ambient soundscapes, subtle frequencies, and minimalist compositions designed to support deep rest, emotional balance, and mindful presence.

With a sensitivity to sound and atmosphere, she creates immersive listening experiences that help individuals slow down, release tension, and reconnect with their inner state.

Through carefully composed audio, Hayley Zhang invites listeners into a calm and supportive space for reflection and everyday stillness.
